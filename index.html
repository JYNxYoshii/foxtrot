The Foxtrot Effort

Who Are We?

We are a small team of about three to four consistent members, and we’re committed to preserving Code Lyoko in the best quality possible. With the usage of kick-ass deep-learning algorithms and cold hard sweat, we will bring you the finest premium USDA-approved viewing experience for Code Lyoko you’ve ever laid eyes on. With all the fixings—subtitles, multiple audio tracks, and bonus features, we can find or make, just like a shiny new DVD or Blu-ray Disc.

Jokes aside, this project was originally created by Christian77777 in late March of 2017 to improve his home server collection of Code Lyoko. This, however, started to turn into a group effort when he asked for help from Alex who had access to higher-end hardware that would quicken the process of improving the video quality. Christian realized other fans of the show would probably enjoy his efforts, so he made a call for help to the Code Lyoko subreddit to provide him with media required to create a meaningful remaster of the show. A humble but determined group of subtitlers and programmers formed, and we decided to name ourselves the Foxtrot Fanatics.

What Are We Doing?

Our project is actually a subset of two projects working together simultaneously.

Transcription Project

  • -Transcribing the show (Mostly manually. YouTube’s automatic captions can be dumb.)
  • -Turning the transcripts into timed subtitles

Upscale Project

  • -Locating the best original video source of the show
  • -Creating a custom automated program: FoxTrotUpscaler™ (trademark pending)
  • -Facilitating the upscaling via FTU from PAL 576i 25fps to 1440p 25fps (4:3 aspect ratio)
  • -Muxing the audio and subtitles with finished video
  • Audio included will be in the following languages:
  • English
  • French

  • Planned Subtitling:
  • English
  • French

All of this will be done on the original episodes, from episode 1 to episode 95 and the two prequel episodes, and possibly a select few other videos (TBD at the discretion of the team).

We will not be performing any work on any other CL works (namely Evolution).

How Can I Get Involved?

More than anything, we could use people with the time and willingness to transcribe. This is the part that takes a lot of time, but we still want to do it, because we know that fans worldwide would love to have them.

No previous background experience is required to become a transcriber. All you need is a few hours out of the week, a keyboard, a computer that can play video, and a willingness to lend a hand. We will fill you in on everything else you need to know to get the job done once you’re in and ready.

If you happen to have any background knowledge in the art of digital video, and feel that you would like to contribute here, you can also contact us for this.

And of course, if there is something else that wasn’t listed here but you think you might want to contribute, do feel free to contact us about that as well.
